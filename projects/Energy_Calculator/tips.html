Quick Energy Saving Tips

LED Lighting

Switch to LED bulbs to use 80% less energy and enjoy up to 25 times longer lifespan compared to traditional bulbs.

Unplug Electronics

Unplug idle electronics to prevent phantom energy consumption and reduce your electricity bill.

Energy Star Appliances

Choose ENERGY STAR certified appliances for optimal energy efficiency and long-term savings.

Cold Water Washing

Use cold water for laundry to save energy and maintain clothing quality.

Common Appliance Energy Usage
