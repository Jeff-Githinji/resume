How It Works

This calculator works by taking the values you input in various fields to work out your potential savings and give you some personalized insights based on your usage.

The energy cost is how much one kilowatt-hour (kWh) of power would cost in your respective location. Please convert it to dollars before you input a value.

The cost savings are calculated by comparing your current energy usage with the optimal usage, assuming 8 hours is optimal for each appliance. The potential savings represent the additional energy you could save by reducing the usage duration beyond the optimal level.

The insights provided are tailored to your usage patterns and suggest ways to optimize your energy consumption, thereby saving you money and reducing your environmental footprint.

Please check the Tips & Recommendations page for some average figures such as the average energy consumption of common appliances and more.
