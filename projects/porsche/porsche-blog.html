The History of Porsche

A journey through Porsche's iconic models

By: Jeff Githinji

A Porsche 911 992

Here's a timeline of Porsche's history:



  • 1931 - Ferdinand Porsche founds Dr. Ing. h.c. F. Porsche GmbH, an engineering consulting firm.
  • 1948 - The first Porsche car, the 356, is produced by Ferdinand's son, Ferdinand "Ferry" Porsche.
  • 1964 - Introduction of the iconic Porsche 911, a model that becomes synonymous with Porsche.
  • 1970 - Porsche wins its first Le Mans 24 Hours race, starting a tradition of racing success.
  • 1984 - Launch of the Porsche 959, a groundbreaking model with advanced technology and performance.
  • 1996 - Introduction of the Porsche Boxster, a model that revives the brand's sports car image.
  • 2002 - Porsche unveils the Cayenne, marking its successful entry into the SUV market.
  • 2015 - The Porsche Mission E concept is introduced, foreshadowing the brand's commitment to electric mobility.
  • 2019 - The Porsche Taycan, Porsche's first all-electric sports car, is launched.

"Porsche is not just a car, it's an icon of automotive history and engineering excellence."
